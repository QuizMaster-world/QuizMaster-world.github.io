QuizMaster Detailed Tutorial

Introduction

Welcome to the QuizMaster tutorial! This guide will help you understand how to use every feature of the QuizMaster application, an open-source alternative to Kahoot. QuizMaster is written in Python and provides a powerful platform for creating and playing educational quizzes.

Home Screen

When you start QuizMaster, you will be greeted by the home screen. From here, you can choose to either play a quiz or create a new quiz.

Click on Play a Quiz to start playing or Make a Quiz to create a new quiz.

Playing a Quiz

To play a quiz, follow these steps:

  1. Click on Play a Quiz from the home screen.
  2. Select a quiz from the list of available quizzes.
  3. Choose the game mode you want to play.
  4. Answer the questions within the time limit (if applicable).
  5. Your score will be displayed at the end of the quiz.

During the quiz, you will see a question and multiple answer options. Click on the correct answer to proceed to the next question.

If you selected the Speed Run mode, try to answer all questions correctly as quickly as possible. In Survival mode, be careful with your answers as you have a limited number of lives.

Creating a Quiz

To create a new quiz, follow these steps:

  1. Click on Make a Quiz from the home screen.
  2. Enter the quiz title and description.
  3. Add questions by clicking on the Add button.
  4. For each question, enter the question text, correct answer, and a set of incorrect answers separated by commas.
  5. Save the quiz once all questions have been added.

Use the interactive GUI to manage and create quiz questions. You can edit, delete, duplicate, open, save, and load quiz questions.

Make sure to provide clear and concise questions and answers to ensure a good quiz experience.

Game Modes

QuizMaster supports multiple game modes:

  • Classic: Answer questions with a countdown timer. Your score is recorded, and at the end, you will receive feedback based on your score.
  • Classic V2: Similar to Classic, but with an absolute time limit instead of individual time limits for each question.
  • Speed Run: Answer all questions correctly in the shortest time possible. Incorrect answers require redoing the question.
  • Survival: Players have a limited number of lives. Each incorrect answer costs a life, and the game ends when all lives are lost.

Select the game mode that best suits your preference and challenge level. Each mode offers a unique experience and can help improve different skills.

Preferences

In the preferences window, you can change the background music, volume, and background color of the application:

Adjust the settings to personalize your QuizMaster experience:

  • Music: Choose from a selection of background music tracks to play during your quiz sessions.
  • Volume: Adjust the volume of the background music to your liking.
  • Background Color: Customize the background color of the application to create a comfortable visual experience.

Additional Features

QuizMaster includes several additional features to enhance your experience:

  • Score Tracking: Your scores are recorded and can be reviewed after each quiz.
  • Feedback: Receive feedback based on your performance in the quiz.
  • Customizable Quizzes: Create and save custom quizzes to challenge yourself and others.
  • Interactive GUI: User-friendly interface for both creating and playing quizzes.
  • Admin Panel: Manage quizzes and users through an admin panel. (Note: This feature may require additional setup and permissions.)
  • Real-time Updates: The application provides real-time updates and feedback during quiz sessions.
  • Multiplayer Support: Play quizzes with friends or classmates in a multiplayer setting. (Note: This feature may be in development or require additional setup.)

Explore these features to get the most out of your QuizMaster experience. Whether you're playing for fun or using it as an educational tool, QuizMaster offers a variety of options to suit your needs.
